Dear Joe Doe,

You recently asked to be removed from the NHS Cervical Screening Programme.

Because of this, we have removed your name from the invitation list. You will not receive any more cervical screening invitations or reminders from the programme.

If you change your mind

Speak to a GP if you want to return to the cervical screening programme and continue receiving invitations.

You can change your mind at any time as long as you are still eligible for cervical screening.


More information

Speak to a GP as soon as possible if you have any of the following symptoms, regardless of your cervical screening history:

  • vaginal bleeding that’s unusual for you – including bleeding during or after sex, between your periods or after the menopause, or heavier periods than usual
  • changes to your vaginal discharge
  • pain during sex
  • pain in your lower back, between your hip bones (pelvis), or in your lower tummy

Find more about cervical screening at: www.nhs.uk/cervical


If you think our information is incorrect or you have any questions, contact a GP or your nearest sexual health clinic.
